Liquid D&B Ratings

The majority of selections on BeatWatch are derived by Chris's exhaustive monthly listening to the Beatport new releases chart, in order to catch any and all of the best tracks to suit his taste.

Chris's approach to ranking is pure self-test of his own enjoyment, along the following scale:

Rating 1 Star (Stale) 2 Star (Proper) 3 Star (Fresh) 4 Star (Massive) 5 Star (Epic)
Would I buy it? Hell no. No. Maybe to make someone else happy. Probably, if I had to. Definitely, if I had to.
How do I feel about it? It's not worth my time. I could take it or leave it. I'd feel OK if I lost it. I'd feel sad if I lost it. I'd feel angry if I lost it.
How much do I like it? I don't like it. Ick. I don't hate it. Meh. I think it's OK. Eh? I think it's good. Nice. I think it's great. Sweet!
How often would I use it (as a DJ)? I wouldn't use it. Ick. I might use it in a DJ set, but not for personal listening. I would use it in a DJ set & for occasional listening. I would use it in a DJ set & for frequent listening. I would use it in a DJ set & for everyday listening.
How often would I enjoy listening to it myself? I wouldn't mind if I never listened to it again. I might enjoy hearing it transiently (inside of a DJ set). I might appreciate hearing a few parts of the tune. I probably appreciate listening to the whole tune. I definitely appreciate listening to the whole tune often.
Who would I share these tunes with? I wouldn't share these tunes (except as a joke). Those who want my "better than average" tunes. Those who want my "best of the season" tunes. Those who want my "best of the year" tunes. Those who want my "best of genre" tunes.
